\## 📌 Project Overview



Ola Ride Insights is a complete end-to-end data analytics project designed to analyze ride-sharing operational data and extract actionable business intelligence.



This project demonstrates:



\- SQL-based data analysis

\- Power BI dashboard development

\- Streamlit web application deployment

\- Business-driven analytical storytelling



---



\## 🎯 Project Objective



The objective of this project is to analyze Ola ride data to:



\- Evaluate ride success performance

\- Identify operational bottlenecks

\- Analyze cancellation trends

\- Measure revenue performance

\- Assess customer \& driver ratings

\- Provide strategic business recommendations

\##  Key Insights



\-  Total Rides: 103,024

\-  Successful Rides: 63,967

\-  Success Rate: 62%

\-  Driver Cancellations: 18,434 (Higher Risk Area)

\-  Customer Cancellations: 10,499

\-  Revenue from Successful Rides: ₹35M+



---



\##  Business Findings



1\. Driver cancellations are significantly higher than customer cancellations.

2\. Revenue is heavily dependent on successful ride completion.

3\. Digital payments (UPI) contribute major revenue share.

4\. Ratings remain stable (~4.0), indicating consistent service quality.



---



\##  Strategic Recommendations



\- Implement driver incentive programs to reduce cancellations.

\- Promote digital payment campaigns to optimize revenue flow.

\- Monitor cancellation trends with real-time dashboards.

\- Introduce loyalty programs to improve ride frequency.
